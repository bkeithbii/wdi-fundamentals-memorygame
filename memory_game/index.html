Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-Suijaku, Pexeso or Simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Overview

Despite what you may think, this is not your average online game. Throughout life your ability to recall certain information and events will be consistently tested. Memory Game 3000 is here for you to sharpen your skills and hone in on how you can begin to remember more and more everyday.
